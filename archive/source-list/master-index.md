## Methodology Notes

1. **English-language bias:** This list prioritises organisations with English-language grant pages. Non-English sources (CNRS/France, DFG/Germany, JST/Japan, Chinese agencies) are noted for potential expansion
2. **Eligibility is dynamic:** Some organisations change eligibility criteria per call — the LLM extraction pipeline must handle per-call rather than per-organisation classification
3. **Verification needed:** Each URL should be tested for crawlability. Some may have changed since compilation (May 2026)
4. **Regional gaps:** Central Asia and Pacific Islands are underrepresented due to limited English-language digital presence — manual supplementation by Jason would be valuable
