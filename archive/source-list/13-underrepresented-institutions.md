# Underrepresented & Non-Resourceful Institutions — Deeper Sources

## Category 13: Under-institutionalised Funding Sources

These are sources that are not "well-known" or "well-resourced" in the traditional sense, but provide active/regular funding opportunities. They are the kind of sources GrantGlobe exists to surface — invisible to existing databases.
